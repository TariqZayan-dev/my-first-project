Our Story

Meet Our Founder

Tariq Khan — CEO & Head Chef

I grew up in a kitchen full of wood-fired aromas and fresh tomatoes. Dubai La Veda is my love letter to simple, seasonal food — made to share. Inspired by my family’s recipes and the vibrant markets of Dubai, I set out to create a place where every meal feels like coming home. Our kitchen celebrates honest ingredients, slow cooking, and the joy of gathering around the table. Whether you’re here for a quick lunch or a long dinner with friends, I hope you taste the care in every bite.
